About

I'm Jakkarin Sonchai (จักริน ศรชัย), a 3D artist based in Ubon Ratchathani, Thailand, with a B.Sc. in Information Technology from Ubon Ratchathani University. I specialise in stylized game characters, weapons and props — modeling, UV mapping, texturing and rigging — including Roblox avatar & UGC character commissions.

At Rievel Games I built game-ready 3D assets end to end: modeling and UV mapping in Blender, texturing in Photoshop and Substance Painter, rigging in Blender, and importing models and animation into Unity. I'm currently open to freelance and full-time 3D roles.
