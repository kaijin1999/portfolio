About

I'm Jakkarin Sornchai (จักริน ศรชัย), a 3D artist based in Ubon Ratchathani, Thailand, specialising in stylized characters, weapons and props — and character rigging. I take pieces from sculpt and retopology through to clean PBR texturing, posing and rig-ready delivery.

I love giving characters clear form, appealing silhouettes and production-ready topology. I'm currently open to freelance and full-time 3D roles.
